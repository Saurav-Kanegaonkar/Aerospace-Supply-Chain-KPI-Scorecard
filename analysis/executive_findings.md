# Executive Findings

## What I analyzed

I joined 5,040 daily metric rows, 760 source events, and 220 action candidates for aerospace supply chain KPI reporting.

## Findings

- The highest-priority supplier part is SUP009 with a priority score of 93.53.
- The top queue combines 51.3 average risk with 79.6 quality score and $773,308 estimated impact.
- The strongest use case is turning repeated stakeholder questions into a ranked operating digest.

## Recommendation

Prioritize the top queue before scaling more dashboards. The project should help leaders decide which supplier part risks need action, validation, or monitoring.
